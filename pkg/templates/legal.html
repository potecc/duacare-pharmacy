Legal & Policies

Important information regarding your privacy and purchases.

Privacy Policy

Customer data is handled with strict confidentiality. All personal, medical, and prescription information provided to Duacare Pharmacy & Stores is securely stored and used solely for service delivery. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Refund Policy

For health and safety compliance, payments are final after a medication has left the pharmacy premises. Refunds and returns are strictly discretionary and will be considered only under exceptional circumstances, such as dispensing errors.

If you have questions regarding a product, please speak to the pharmacist immediately upon pick-up or delivery.
