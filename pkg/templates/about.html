About Duacare Pharmacy

Duacare Pharmacy & Stores is a fast-growing community pharmacy located in Benin City, Nigeria. Founded by a licensed Pharmacist, Duacare was established to bridge the gap between accessible healthcare, professional pharmaceutical services, and everyday household needs.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, reliable, and affordable healthcare while supporting everyday wellness for individuals and families.

Our Vision

To become a leading tech-enabled pharmacy and wellness brand across Southern Nigeria.

Community Impact

Duacare actively supports community health education, preventive care initiatives, affordable access to medicines, and job creation within the local community.
